How to use?

Place your cursor on the barcode field, Now you can scan and it will withdraw the item. If you want to delete by manual typing , you may type and press the delete button.

Parameters

  • Override:Indication whether the item should be deleted even if warnings exist. Optional. By default: false
  • Holdings:Method for handling a Holdings record left without any items: retain, delete or suppress. Optional. By default: retain.
